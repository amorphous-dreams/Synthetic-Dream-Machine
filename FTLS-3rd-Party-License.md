## I. Purpose

Elyncia runs on remix culture. The DreamNet was built on salvage, synthesis, and the contributions of thousands of operators who fed the shrines, extended the protocols, and filed their own amendments to the noosphere. We want Fan Works — house rules, supplements, campaign settings, fiction, filks, tools, and AI agent forks. We want to promote the best of them to official status. We want the Elyncia platform at [elyncia.app](https://elyncia.app) to be a living ecosystem of community-built content, not a walled garden.

This license lets you build on *Flying Triremes and Laser Swords* (FTLS), the Elyncia setting, the Lares agent architecture, and all other original Amorphous Dreams Cabal content in this repository — for free, for non-commercial purposes — without waiting on our review or approval.

In the same spirit as the Synthetic Dream Machine 3rd Party License by Luka Rejec, which governs the SDM rules ecosystem we build on, this license removes barriers and asks only a few things in return.

Make it weird, wonderful, and wild.
