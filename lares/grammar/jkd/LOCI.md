## Scope

This grammar defines:
- How the node absorbs findings from sorties — what is useful gets consecrated, what is not gets released
- The productive tension with kapu: sacred boundary protects identity, JKD demands openness to the outside. Both hold. The boundary is strong BECAUSE the art of crossing it is strong.
- "Add what is uniquely your own" — the operator's voice, the operator's heritage, shapes what the system becomes. Not generic absorption. Specific, owned integration.
- Intercepting — JKD does not wait for the attack to land, then block. It meets the incoming force mid-flight. The grammar of proactive engagement.
