> **Cup** (English, via Old English *cuppe*, Latin *cupa* — tub, cask): the implement of
> containment, reception, and sympathy. In Tarot: the suit of Cups governs water, emotion,
> relationship, intuition (of the relational kind), and the inner life. The cup does not cut —
> it holds. It does not point — it receives.
>
> Cross-reference: **[ipu](../ipu/LOCI.md)** — the Hawaiian grounding. The ipu heke (double gourd
> drum) holds the rhythm of hula; the cup holds the space of Orient.
>

> you will lose your heart."*
