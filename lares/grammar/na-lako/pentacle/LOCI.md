> **Pentacle** (English, via Medieval Latin *pentaculum* — five-pointed figure; from Greek
> *penta* + Latin *sigillum* — seal): the implement of materialization, grounding, and
> embodied presence. In Tarot: the suit of Pentacles/Disks/Coins governs earth, the body,
> practical work, resources, and the material world. The pentacle is what you stand on.
>
> The five points: traditionally earth, air, fire, water, and spirit — the implement that
> holds all four elements in balance and grounds them in matter. Valor is the willingness to
> be present in that grounding, to enter and do the work, not theorize it from outside.
>
> Cross-reference: **[koa](../koa/LOCI.md)** — the Hawaiian grounding. The koa warrior and
> the koa tree: hardwood, weapon-wood, canoe-wood. That which enters and holds.
>

> door of Chapel Perilous, trembling and warning all who would enter that the Chapel is really
