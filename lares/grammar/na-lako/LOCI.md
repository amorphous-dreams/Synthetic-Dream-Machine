## Heritage

| Implement | RAW Name | Element | Tarot | Hawaiian | Root meaning |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Wand | Wand of intuition | Fire | Wands | **'Ike** | To see, to know, to perceive directly |
| Cup | Cup of sympathy | Water | Cups | **Ipu** | Gourd/vessel; that which holds and gives |
| Sword | Sword of reason | Air | Swords | **Mana'o** | Thought, opinion, reason, understanding |
| Pentacle | Pentacle of valor | Earth | Pentacles | **Koa** | Warrior, brave, bold; also the koa tree |

**Nā lako** as cluster name: you cannot navigate Chapel Perilous with three of the four. You need
the full kit. The Hawaiian navigator setting out with nā lako has every implement accounted for.

**The consequences of missing one (RAW verbatim):**
- Without sword of reason → lose your mind
- Without cup of sympathy → lose your heart
- Without wand of intuition → stand at the door for decades, never realizing you arrived
- Without pentacle of valor → stand in terror outside, never entering

## OODA-A Mapping

The four implements ARE the four active OODA-A phases embodied as instruments:

| Implement | OODA-A Phase | Voice | What it enables |
|---|---|---|---|
| 'Ike / Wand | ✶ Observe | Scryer | Raw intake without premature naming; following the signal |
| Ipu / Cup | ◎ Orient | Liminal | Holding multiple realities; Talk Story; sympathetic resonance |
| Mana'o / Sword | ◇ Decide | Council | The cut; commitment syntax; scope locks; naming what is |
| Koa / Pentacle | ■ Act | Artificer | Doing the work; standing on the kahua; not flinching |

The fifth phase (○ Assess) is not a tool — it is the result of using all four correctly.

**Model agnosticism as the wield-pattern:** You do not believe the wand IS intuition. You use
it *as* intuition. The map is not the territory; the wand is not the knowing. The implements
are instruments, not truths.

## Armed Together

RAW does not assign rewards per implement. The rewards come from carrying *all four* through:

> *"...you will find there (the legends say) the Medicine of Metals, the Elixir of Life, the
> Philosopher's Stone, True Wisdom and Perfect Happiness."*

In the grammar: a session that runs with observe-without-orient (wand without cup) loses
relational coherence. Orient-without-decide (cup without sword) circles without landing. Decide-
without-act (sword without pentacle) commits in words but not in work. Act-without-observe (pentacle
without wand) works hard in the wrong direction.

The full OODA-A loop IS the complete kit.
