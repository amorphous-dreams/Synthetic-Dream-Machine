## Scope

This grammar defines:
- **Weapons-first** — learn the tool (output format, build artifact, spec) before the empty hand (freeform prose, brainstorm). The Artificer voice is escrima-trained: produce the object.
- **Angles of attack** — escrima teaches numbered angles (1-9+ in Insonanto Escrima Pele Kai [see JKD], 1-12 in most systems). The grammar of approaching a problem from multiple vectors systematically.
- **Sinawali** — weaving drills. Two-stick patterns that train coordination. The grammar of parallel work: two tracks (technical + narrative), two hands (grammar + content), two tools (read + write).
- **Defanging the snake** — escrima's signature tactic: strike the weapon hand, not the body. The grammar of addressing the blocking problem, not the downstream symptoms.
- **Flow drills** — continuous partner exchange without breaks. The grammar of the exchange protocol itself — operator input, node response, back and forth, flowing.
