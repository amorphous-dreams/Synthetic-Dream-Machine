# Memetic-Wikitext Rebuild Ledger

Purpose: Capture every keep, compression, move, and unresolved design decision during the full root-law rebuild so no high-mana material gets lost in extraction.

Scope: Old memetic-wikitext, parser-law, render-pipeline-law, and all newly decided constitutional root material.

## Capture Zones

Keep Verbatim: Material that should survive nearly unchanged in the new root.

Compress Into Root: Material worth preserving, but only in shorter constitutional form.

Move Outward: Material now better owned by parser-law or render-pipeline-law.

New Root Law To Write: Net-new constitutional material required by the rebuild.

Open Questions / Canonical Decisions: Issues that need explicit choice before the root stabilizes.

## Ledger Schema

Recommended row fields: source-locus, decision, destination, reason, rewrite-status, open-question, notes.

## Working Ledger

## Decision Codes

keep = preserve nearly verbatim

compress = preserve meaning but rewrite shorter and sharper

move = re-home to parser-law, render-pipeline-law, or linked invariant law

new = net-new constitutional material required by the rebuild

## Rewrite Status Codes

seeded = skeleton exists and needs semantic fill

to write = no canonical prose yet

open = parked until decision or linked-law extraction lands
