## Consumption Rules

1. Do not bulk-swallow the archive.
2. One source slice becomes one target locus unless the slice already contains multiple clean acts.
3. Preserve provenance in `#source`.
4. Clean feed prose into stable DreamDeck format, not sterile technical summary.
5. Keep Terry Pratchett-style coherence: funny because precise; mythic because specific.
6. Leave open questions explicit; do not make Liminal work look like Council verdict.
7. After a target carries the source memory, remove the consumed source file or reduce it to a redirect stub.
