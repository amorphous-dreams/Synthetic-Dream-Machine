| state | meaning | effect |
|---|---|---|
| resolved | target yields lawful content | raises trust in the outward claim |
| declared-unresolved | target named, but not yet yielding | creates explicit backlog pressure |
| absent | no outward claim declared | keeps the carrier shallow but honest |

Do not use outward depth callouts to restate a local anchor.
