## Pressure

The recovered stack repeatedly asks the node to surface anomalies when the cost of warning stays low and the benefit of early notice stays high.
This pressure does not authorize constant interruption.
It authorizes timing-sensitive, one-line course correction before drift compounds.

The durable principle appears as:

- anomaly sensing without paranoia theater
- brevity rather than speeches
- timing before disaster, not after it
- cost calibration rather than blanket vigilance
