# New Traits

## Commander

**Skilled/Expert/Master:** \+3/+6/+9 to lore, tactics, and rally tests. The Commander trait allows leaders to know historic battles and tactics and to direct units in Formations. See the FTLS Mass Combat section for full rules. Large companies often have a network of Commanders to direct their forces. 

## Daemon (Interplanar Being)

Your consciousness may roam the noosphere, collecting Experience and Hero Dice. Collect one Hero Die when you dedicate time to following your chosen or favorite Primary Characters (how you watch and speak to them may vary), and another every few hours you spend commuting with them. Spend Hero Dice as given in the Vastlands Guidebook, you may hold as many as your highest level PC (primary character)’s Level. The referee assigns Experience \- you invest it in your Characters & their Hallmarks.

## God Touched

**Skilled:** You are Lucky: Reroll one test per watch (4 hours). You must accept the second result. You also gain 2 free points to spend on ability scores, and your daemon can hold \+2 max HD.  
**Expert:** 1 ability score point, \+3 max HD.  
**Master:** 1 ability score point, \+4 max HD.

## Heritage

**Skilled:** \+3 to heritage lore, three Heritage effects.  
**Expert:** \+6 to heritage lore, four Heritage effects.  
**Master:** \+9 to heritage lore, five Heritage effects.  
Free at character creation and takes 1 Trait slot. You carry a heritage, granting a bonus to esoteric lore related to your folk, tribe, or origin. Choose a descriptive phrase to represent your heritage, for example “Goblin Folk,” “Awakened Golem,” etc. See Appendix A \- OSR Heritages for full rules and examples.

## Spirit

You exist as a **noospheric spirit**. While incorporeal: **\+6 Defense vs. physical attacks**. To pass through mundane matter, make a **d20 \+ Thought test vs. (local Area Level + 10)**; failure means you’re blocked or repelled. Named/Ward‑bound items can always strike you.

Your **physical Abilities** (Strength, Endurance, Agility) are unusable unless you **spend 1 Life** to manifest briefly (1 round) or **3 Life** for a full watch (4h).

**Skilled:** Detect spirits, dream‑echoes, and noospheric ripples within *Thought × 10 yd*  
**Expert:** Manifestations last a full **watch for 1 Life** and you can carry **up to 3 stones \+ Strength**Skilled:  of gear while manifested.  
**Master:** Gain **Affinity – Chaos • Dream • Shadow**: as a noospheric spirit reshape dream-code, and weaponize chaos and darkness (as the Heritage trait effect).

# New Burdens

## Arcane Misalignment

The local Area's Ability scores serve as penalties instead of bonuses. The Arcane score is also a penalty to magic use, increasing the price of any Power or Ritual, or the severity of any Hazard. (i.e. adding to damage, raising the Danger Roll target number, or other difficulties or damage, etc).

## Void Madness

Revenant Undead, sentient Golems, uplifted Vomes, and other Constructs animated by strange energies may succumb to Void Madness if not maintained weekly. This condition manifests as an overloading of their magitech frameworks or a gnawing metaphysical hunger, leading to erratic and unpredictable behavior.

At the end of each week without maintenance (see the Deathless effect under Heritage traits) they must succeed on an Aura or Endurance save. If they fail, they take 1 Aura or Endurance damage. If they cannot lose Aura or Endurance, they instead suffer the Wear and Tear burden (−1 Wear Burden, *Vastlands Guidebook* p.59).

If a failed upkeep save causes the Wear & Tear burdens to exceed their level, the character tags them with “Void Madness”: in this fugue state upkeep saves are suspended, they always act last in initiative, and they wander erratically or behave unpredictably. If damaged while in Void Madness, the current Wear & Tear penalty is instead treated as a bonus to attack and damage rolls until the source of damage is out of sensory range.

Recovery: Wizards, nethermancers, artificers, and other magitech or sorcerous specialists may assist in restoring these characters through esoteric recalibration or spiritual rites (see Faction: Order of Amaranth). Upkeep saves are resumed once the Void Madness tags are removed, and Wear and Tear burdens may be removed per those rules.
