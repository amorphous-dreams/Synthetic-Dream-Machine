# Wild Magic

## Violet ozone, upside‑down rain, vox feedback

The unstable ley line networks of Elyncia hold a startling secret. Anyone who knows the rites can pull power out of the ambient mana. This is not mentioned around those without an appropriate magitech (oldtech) or sorcery (fantascience) skill, and rarely logged in archived info. The rites and secrets are varied but the pattern remains stable: sacrifice your action to meditate on the local web of magical energy.

## Dangerous Magic

A Danger Roll is required when a referee indicates the presence of \[dangerous\] magic, a character activates a Power with a power price higher than their level, or when facing Hazards triggered by actions such as Recon, Salvage, or Secrets.

To make a **Danger Roll**:  
\> d20 \+ Ability (usually Aura) \+ Skill (if applicable) vs power (mana price)

When a spell mishap, narrative cue, or failed Danger Roll occurs, the acting character immediately gains a minor burden (such as a dramatic nosebleed or headache), and the referee rolls an exposure test (2d6 + Aura, VLG p 98).
