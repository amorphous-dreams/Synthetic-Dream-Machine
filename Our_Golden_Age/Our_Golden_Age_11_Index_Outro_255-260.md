**Other myths of the Mud Sculptor**

She stole the source of life from paradise and set it loose once more in the Given World.

Nay, she worked in concert with the other Potentialities, creating life in consensus.

This creation was accidental, a workshop accident as she wrestled with the challenges of the demons Boredom and Curiosity.

It was not accidental, she created life within her halls of labouring, but we escaped, much as told in the tale of Whitefur and Bighead.

She created life anew to bring chaos back to the crystal perfection of paradise.

The recreation of life was planned all along, but the other Potentialities held her back, stopped her from enacting it in the Given World for an entire aeon.

**Prophecies of the Mud Sculptor**

She will return again in a furious fire if the other Potentialities try to scrape away mud-born life as their silver angels scrape dirt off their taloned feet.

When life discovers the truths of form and song that underpin creation, she will take the chosen up to the eternal paradise, where there is no discord or strife.

When life reaches its apex and produces the Seven Heroes of the End, she will descend with her brother Rat, the unmaker of harvests, the reaper of tyrants, the dancer of the dawn.

Together they will recruit the seven to climb the Seven Pillars of Heaven and rekindle the stalled engines of perfection.

She waits even now, frozen in her moment of apotheosis, awaiting her consort re evolved from life restored.

After the second beginning of life, the other Potentialities exiled her beyond the Opal Blast Door of reality. But a hero will evolve and bring her back.

After life began, she remembered why this story was such necessary folly. After five aeons of stewarding life, she grew tired and left it alone for a few hours to get some rest.

When she awoke again, she found the other gods had wreaked havoc, some calling fish their own, others birds, yet others the rodents called humans. Laughing at the absurdity, she revealed herself to the Doors of Creation and walked out on the Given World along the Garden Paths.
