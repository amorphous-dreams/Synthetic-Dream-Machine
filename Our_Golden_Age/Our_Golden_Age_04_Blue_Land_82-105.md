### BLUE LAND

> [@OGA, _p._ _82_]

<!-- Our Golden Age, Page 083 -->

_"Dead gods, soarstones, living rains, and endless liberty."_ —Belico Chaparral, reintegration prophet

It seems the Blue Land was where the Dream came alive, a place of creativity and innovation, where the [illegible] adapted the best of Heaven to the merest of the Humans.

Sadly the old histories were corrupted by the Incident (War?) and current theories on what this meant are in disagreement. Was it a holy pleasure preserve? A radical edenoforming zone? A labor designation for lobogods?

Some modern authorities, such as the High Realist of the Svat of Hexad Foreign Affairs, suggest that Blue is just a geographical expression, a borderland between the oldtech might of the Green and the mystical fantascience of the Purple.

As it is, the Sacred Reintegration Protocols (SRP) divide the traditional lower Blue among the externally administered UCC, CUC, SEZ, and various subsidiary polities—viz. Azula, Indigo West, Mur and UVS. The upper Blue is maintained as the Designation, an independent monitored territory for surviving Bluelanders.

Though popular imagination still holds this to be a fallen land, roving with chimerical horrors suitable only for disaster tourism, the SRP has made it one of the most dynamic region of the Rainbowlands, with scavenging conglomerates and renewtech startups transforming human possibilities in ways unthinkable for generations. If you want to get a head, this is the place to be!

**Climate**

The great Halt Blank (Alto Blanco in the local patois) to the north caps the realm in ice and air so thin unassisted human lungs must shrivel. The deep-carving rivers ensure winter's chill is ever but a hike away.

The low-lying valleys are drenched year-long, swathed in fogs and cloudbursts from the ageless rainmakers humidifying the world as the Builders intended. Occasionally, aerolith towers or fossils of the Blue God crumble, unleashing landslides, floods, and miasmas.

**Population**

reintegration territories: ~1.5 million, designation territories: ~0.2–1.2 million

**Groups**

new bluelanders 28%, old bluelanders 23%, dis-integrated laborers 17%, greenlanders 13%, free purplelanders 9%, purplelanders 6%, others 4%.

**Languages**

Canopic (official), Blaunestalk (suppressed), RD Cant (unofficial), Neo-Azulic (trade interlanguage), Perimse (river traders), Free Pura (unclear).

**Capital**

Official: pending reconstruction. Practical: Special Economic Zone has been designated a New Model City and will feature amenities unheard of, such as hot and cold running plumbing, free indoor sunlight lamps, and edible mycelial wall panelling. It is currently 6.2% finished and funding is being secured!

**Government**

Polycondominium concession zones. Radically overlapping competing administrations use market signals and the Invisible Hand ideology to maximize uplift returns. The Designation is probably an autarkic subsistence-level zone of human disaster settlement.

**Ideologies**

Cogflower: regrowth (supported), Cathedral: innovator (free), Blue God: cave horror (suppressed), Free Industrialism: honorary (imported).

**Economy**

"But then the [illegible] went False and embraced the Error and the War followed. Devastation," mutters the Econotabulator, mad in its oversight bunker. Thus, the modern Blue Land economy is left to its own devices.

**Resources**

bacterial cultures, bio-autogenerators, dairy, lamarckian modulators, godsflesh, aerolith structures, living bone.

**Means of Production**

Until reintegration is complete, private enterprise is encouraged. Bluelander ownership is discouraged for obvious security reasons.

**Material Conditions**

Official: each registered laborer is entitled to a personalized living dormisac and 10 cash per week. Effective: black market research suggests reliable laborers uncorrupted by the various error plagues command 28 to 35 cash per week.

#### BLUE POWER GROUPS

_"Warning! The region is under an open-ended reintegration interdiction. Visitors without official peacemaker authorization will be forcibly disembodied."_ —City Azure Ordinator, Diktat 772g

Countless peace memorials celebrate the recent and very permanent and eternal liberation of the Blue Land. Under the Sacred Reintegration Protocols all factions are outlawed, therefore this section refers to power groups.

There are three basic types of power group: the external administrations with their concession zones, the internal subsidiary groups affiliated with the various administrations, and the independent power groups tolerated in the Designation.

The Ruins Azure area is a special case, as it is run independently of the various power groups by the City Azure Ordinator, the local administrative demigod brought over to reintegrate the area, and its three special economic zones, which promise a socio-cultural efflorescence of the area. The fourth special economic zone mentioned in older documents is erroneous. There is no such zone. There is no alienation zone.

**Peacemaker Companies**

Two major peacemaker companies, sent from the cities Emerald and Violet and headed by appointed governors, both claim to be rebuilding the Blue Land with their apparatuses, armies and coteries of capitalist collaborators. Secondary peacemaker companies are uplifting smaller sections of the Blue Land on concession.

- **Union of Cities Congress Peacemaker Companies (UCCPC).** Originally an Emerald City outfit, the UCCPC produces glossy reports on the progress and growth of its zones. For 17 years the ratio of pink synthent per person has grown by an average of 20% per annum, meaning that today every UCCPC resident is eligible for roughly 20,000 calories of high-joy nutritional fuel per day. All the stumbling malnourished gaunts (L0, lamentable) are actors hailing the good name of the UCCPC.

- **The liberated Bluelanders call the UCCPC "Coins".**

- **Akila / Vilgare VI.** The latest governor-appointee is due any month now. By then the local secretary of accounts, Sol Perceiver, needs vaults as full as his ledgers. Good jobs are available for tax farmers who will go the extra mile for their 30% commission.

- **Congress of United Cities Peacemaker Companies (CUCPC).** The Violet City derived CUCPC produces elegant, minimalist poetry on the purification and development of its cantons. Its residents have grown 17% mentally purer each year, leaving them 99.9% enlightened. Any sad faces are recent illegals.

   The liberated Bluelanders call the CUCPC "Cups".

- **Sad Molakyo and Skullwaygyles.** The current governor is a bloated waiting with a post-mortal cat lord. Skullwaygyles needs a new body and a better pet. That may explain the covert cat pride investigating the cantons to see if the purifications poems are correct ... and if there is a large crop of healthy new pets available for the Violet City and the local administration. Local despots ... er ... officers will pay top cash card for mind-scrubbers who can help them quickly rip a new crop of pets.

_In truth, neither the Eastern City nor the Western City rules, while the old local capital Azure is a ruin surrounded by a barely administered hinterland._

**Free Residents, Ruinsdwellers**

The post-Blue melange of locals and arrivals drawn to the Ruins Azure, with its special economic zones. An anomic, post-communal, swirling, vibrant, individualistic mob focused on one thing alone: wealth. From scavengers to rebuilders, wildcat bankers to post-legal biomancers, every kind of hare-brained scheme can blossom on the wealth promised by the fossil remnants of the Dead God.

One who enjoys the refinement and proprieties of a civilized social hierarchy would do well to avoid the free residents. A more disturbingly vulgar and materialistic society can hardly be found anywhere else in the Rainbowlands. Not even in that hive of the accountant monks, the Emerald City.

- _Piet' Gagosi._ Scrap merchant, fine fellow and not at all a mobster. Got a lead from a baked-out sporey. **Aerolith Cloudlands**

_high-elevation rainmaker ecosystem_

The rainmakers of the Designation blow aerolith grains into the air and clouds, which blooms of air plankton—tiny animals, plants, and stranger animalcules—harvest to build the shells and cell walls that allow them to float free of the World's embracing gravity.

Many blooms are so dense and long-lived that their bodies accrete, anchoring around aerolith spires or Long Ago gravitic tethers, creating billowing aerial islands of crumbly matter—the cloudlands. Mushrooms and lichens knit these terrains together, creating a realm where the Blue God's corruption springs from every shadow, erupts with every bacillus, festers in every dairy product, sings in the very air. It is also home to breeding colonies of **flappers** (L3, wild) and even **air-dugongs** (L8, luminous).

**Fungale Tower Houses** _upcycled oldtech citadels, detritolite settlements_

The High Blue and Starwatcher Heights are especially rich in accreted detritolite, that scrapstone of fossilized machinery and half-finished products that draws miners like moths to the flame. Some claim that these towers, hauling themselves out of the lithomachinery laced mountains, have a particular affinity with the wizard cultists of the Dead God, but this is unproven.

These sky-piercing heights call to every mountaineer and terrorize every profiteer, creating a challenging and half-demented aerial geometry of switchback valleys, towering mountains, and aerolith skylands. Each tower house is different, an extraction of its own oddness:

1. Thick with memory slabs, stones engraved by hand with records of forgotten wars, barely comprehensible equations, and exhortations to fight against the fading of the history of mankind.

3. A mad accretion of components and parts and wings and struts and propellers, an obsessive hoarder's idea of all that would be required to reclaim the air.
4. A vertical garden, a tree of trees, clumped together with stone and bone and aerolith, straining to reach the Green Sun, withering in the thin upper air.
5. The hands and feet and shoulders and backs of géants, torn apart, reassembled, creating a kind of climbing, crushing, opening anti-machine.
6. Glistering scales, twinkling wires, jewels of mind and personality, the memory store of a Dead God?
