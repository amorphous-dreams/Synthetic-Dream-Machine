# Lady Aki — Discounted Gear Ledger

*FTLS / SDM+ alpha playtest. Powers remain deferred.*

## Accepted Alpha Rulings

- **Ruffle-Coat comparator accepted** for Aki's primary robe/armor layer.
- **Moss Hat reskin updated** to **Veiled Miko Work Hat / Ichimegasa with Uchikatsugi Veil**.
- Gear may be **re-skinned** for Spider Folk / shrine / mycelial culture while preserving canonical SDM+ price, rules, bulk, and mechanical effect.
- Reskin entries retain the **canonical basis** so later audits can trace mechanics back to SDM+.
- New Delos stabilizes prices around SDM+ book prices and widens availability of uncommon/rare+ items.
- **Adventurer's Guild discount:** 15%.
- **Spider Folk starting heritage discount:** 50%.
- **Stacked discount:** list price x 0.50 x 0.85 = 42.5% of list price.
- **Buckets:** On Body / In Shrine / Company Pool = Company Ship Cargo.
- Broader ship cargo remains on the gorgonne captain or shared company sheet.

### On Body Notes

- **Veiled Miko Work Hat** replaces the previous **Spore-Mother Moss Cap** label. It keeps the Moss Hat rules exactly but expresses them through traveling-miko protective dress: veil, ritual modesty, dust/sun/insect protection, and soft anonymity.

# Neo-Thracia Friction Notes

The Lindwyrm has sponsored the company into the Adventurer's Guild at rank 1. The company carries basic ID prismatic keys.

In wider Nexus culture, these keys may grant:
- legitimacy,
- quartermaster access,
- paper cover,
- Guild discounts,
- introductions,
- salvage authority.

In Neo-Thracia, roughly five centuries of isolation make them socially unstable. They may:
- mark the company as useful outsiders,
- trigger suspicion,
- imply old imperial/noospheric authority,
- attract rival outsiders,
- fail among local groups with no living Guild practice,
- or become valuable proof of connection to New Delos.

Use Guild credit confidently in New Delos and with Guild-aware captains. Use it cautiously after landfall.

# Design Read

This build tests the FTLS alpha questions we care about:

1. Do Spider Folk cultural discounts feel powerful without breaking the economy?
2. Does re-skinning canonical SDM+ gear preserve table trust?
3. Does Aki's shrine/gear layer support the whole company without stealing other PCs' niches?
4. Does Ichi/Shrine maintenance become a meaningful budget pressure?
5. Does New Delos availability make uncommon gear useful while Neo-Thracia keeps social friction alive?
6. Does the miko-travel silhouette clarify Aki's social role better than generic fungal headwear?

Current answer: yes, provisionally.
