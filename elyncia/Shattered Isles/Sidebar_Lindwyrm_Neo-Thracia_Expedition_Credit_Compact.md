# Sidebar — Lindwyrm Expedition Credit Compact v2

Each Player-Daemon receives:
- 150 cash from SDM starting character rules,
- 500 cash as Lindwyrm loan,
- five 1-Orichalcum Guild certificates worth 100 cash each in Adventurer's Guild credit.

Repayment:
- expected within one year,
- separate from mission rewards,
- may be renegotiated through coin, relics, maps, salvage rights, routes, favors, or political leverage.

## Guild Cover

The Lindwyrm sponsors the company into the Adventurer's Guild at rank 1.

The company carries basic ID prismatic keys.

These may grant:
- legitimacy,
- quartermaster access,
- Guild discounts,
- paper cover,
- introductions,
- salvage authority.

## Neo-Thracia Friction

Neo-Thracia's long isolation makes Guild markers socially unstable.

They may:
- help with New Delos outsiders,
- confuse local communities,
- imply foreign noospheric authority,
- attract rival claimants,
- or signal useful connections.
