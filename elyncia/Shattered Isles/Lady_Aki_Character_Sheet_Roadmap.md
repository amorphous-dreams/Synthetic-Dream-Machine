# Lady Aki — Character Sheet Roadmap

*FTLS / SDM+ alpha playtest.*

## Core Identity

Lady Aki is a wandering Spider Folk miko-wizard, fungal alchemist, illusionist, shrine keeper, and DreamNet field operator attached to a Lindwyrm-backed Neo-Thracian expedition company.

She travels with:
- **Ichi**, a Powered Symbiote Hallmark companion,
- the **Faerie-Ring Strandbeest-Shrine**,
- **Ephyra** tasked spirits,
- allied PCs, hallmarks, crew, mounts, pets, and expedition infrastructure.

## Design Truth

Aki has three visual registers:

1. **True Body** — iridescent fae-chitin, Spider Folk extra arms, soft alien face, chitinous plates, web/silk physiology.
2. **Public Glamour** — a wandering miko silhouette: red/white shrine clothing, silksteel travel robes, classic veiled ichimegasa, and soft glamour that makes her chitinous form socially legible.
3. **Threshold / Danger Reveal** — glamour thins; extra arms, chitin, thread-lines, bells, and ward geometry become obvious. If overt violence breaks out, Ichi becomes a suit of mobile powered armor - Aki steps back or into his embrace and a system greater than its parts emerges.

**Faerie glamour note:** Aki's public presentation hides or softens her chitinous form. This is primarily a social/visual veil unless a specific Power or roll makes it mechanically active. It does not erase her true Spider Folk body; it mediates how outsiders perceive it.

# Heritage — Spider Folk

* Skilled (+3): **Affinity – Silk & Web, Alternate Movement – Climb, Armoured Body**

## Affinity - Silk & Web

Tags: \[illusion\] \[silk\] \[web\]

Spin lines, sheets, glamours, and snares from web‑silk. Weave fashion, harness, sail, or trap from a thought and a spool; reroute pursuit with sticky geometry and elegant pranks.

## Alternate Movement

Gain a second movement mode (Climb, Swim, Fly, Hover, Burrow, etc.), and move at your normal Speed. After 30 minutes of continuous use, mark your Heritage trait and make a Wear and Tear attrition save (VLG p. 59). While marked, make another attrition save every 10 minutes you use the alternate movement type.

## Armoured Body

\+1 Armor and \+1 Ward. Select a second time to raise the bonuses to \+2/+2.

Fictional permissions:
- read web tension, silk patterns, vibration, and shrine-thread omens,
- treat webbing as ritual medium, alchemical binder, ward-line, or illusion scaffold,
- negotiate with spider-fae, web spirits, shrine daemons, and threshold things from a position of kinship.

Discount doctrine:
- Spider Folk starting heritage discount: 50% on clearly associated starting purchases.
- Strong candidates: silk, webwork, ritual thread systems, illusion-stage fabrics, web-climbing systems, shrine-textile infrastructure.
- The discount reflects cultural access and kin-supply, not universal coupon logic.
