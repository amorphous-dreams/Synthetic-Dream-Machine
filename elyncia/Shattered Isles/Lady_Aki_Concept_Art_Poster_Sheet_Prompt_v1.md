# Lady Aki — Poster-Sized Landscape Concept Sheet Prompt v1

Use this as an art-generation / art-direction prompt.

## Prompt

Full poster-sized landscape-oriented character design sheet for **Lady Aki**, a wandering Spider Folk miko-wizard, fungal alchemist, illusionist, and shrine-field operator in a mythpunk science-fantasy tabletop RPG. Style: video game design concept art sheet, exploratory, sketch annotations, turnaround elements, detail callouts, not final polished key art.

Show multiple design states on one wide sheet:

- **Primary full-body public glamour:** Aki wearing red/white miko work robes under silksteel ritual layers, classic wide-brimmed **ichimegasa** travel hat with long **uchikatsugi-style veil**, shrine bells, ofuda, silk cords, spinneret crochet/web toolkit at sash, wandering priestess silhouette. Her faerie glamour hides or softens her chitinous Spider Folk body.
- **True body / glamour-thin inset:** iridescent violet, turquoise, and green fae chitin; extra arms; gentle alien face; natural armor plates; elegant Spider Folk anatomy, sacred and fae rather than monstrous.
- **Action/threshold silhouette:** veil lifted, extra arms anchoring silk lines and ofuda, bell-tikker charm, ward geometry, controlled ritual posture.
- **Ichi companion:** compact humanoid ashigaru powered armor / spore-daemon construct, medium stocky sergeant silhouette, chitin kikko plates, dark lacquer, glowing orange daemon-spore eye-orbs, mycelium-silk backing, cracks filled with orange bioluminescent scar-tissue. **No spider legs on Ichi.**
- **Faerie-Ring Strandbeest-Shrine background:** mobile roadside shrine / hokora on six to eight articulated strandbeest legs, silk-and-mycelium sails, orange-glowing Mother Spore fairy ring, blue glassy Ephyra spore-jellies, armored wagon-bed, expedition cargo.
- **Expedition scale inset:** Prismatic Elf ranger friend with large grimalkin-lynx mount near the shrine, small compared to the mobile lararium.

Mood: mythpunk, SDM / Vastlands-inspired weird fantasy, shrine-tech, oldtech-magitech, fae glamour, field logistics, sacred threshold worker. Palette: red and white miko robes, purple outer cloth, iridescent chitin, orange mycelium glow, weathered shrine wood/chitin, blue Ephyra glass.

Composition: landscape poster concept sheet with full-body views, rough turnaround silhouettes, material swatches, gear callouts, handwritten design notes, arrows, thumbnails, scale silhouettes.

## Negative Prompt / Avoid

No spider legs on Ichi. No tall ritual hat. No generic witch hat. No polished final illustration. No single pinup pose. No hypersexual framing. No modern tactical sci-fi armor replacing ashigaru/shrine language. No hiding the miko identity. No making the mobile shrine into Ichi. No making Aki a generic human without chitin reveal callouts.
